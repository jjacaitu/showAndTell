An image classifier use neural networks and machine learning to recognize the content of images based on the images it was trained with.

Posenet and SoundClassifier

SoundClassifier allows you to classify audio by using the "SpeechCommands18w" model that lets you recognize words like the ten digits from "zero" to "nine", "up", "down", "left", "right", "go","stop", "yes" or "no".

Posenet is a model that uses naural networks to identify the pose of a user, it can detect certain points of the body like the nose, eyes, elbows, knees, etc.
